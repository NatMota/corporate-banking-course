Course description 
==================

This course shall introduce students to business processes in corporate 
banking. We shall look at the firm financing decisions and role of bank loans,  
structure of loan agreements as well as bank prоcesses that support deal orgination, 
monitoring and bad loan resolution. The students should be able to judge upon firm 
creditworthiness, come up with loan termsheet and analyse a portfolio of loans. Addtional 
knowledge will be gained on bank products and functional roles within corporate financing 
in a bank. We also expect certain level of proficiency in navigating of bank sector and 
related statistics in different geographies upon completion of the course.

Description of course methodology
=================================

[You can include a brief description of your teaching methods/techniques and provide

We have data for quantatitive case studies - a corporate reports dataset, 
bank statistics dataset and a several countries releases of flow of funds statistics. 
We will use this data as lecture material and in homework/excercises.

Students are expected to formulate their end-to-end model of corporate bank process
including a portfolio loans, firm statistics and bank balance sheet/P&L. Students shall 
be able to adjust the depth of the model according to their interest in particular parts 
of deal lifecycle.
